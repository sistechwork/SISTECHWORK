CRM + HRM

The VHomifi Suite

Where CRM Meets HRM in a Unified Intelligence Portal.

The Vision

In the traditional business model, Sales (CRM) and Operations (HRM) exist in separate universes. This disconnection leads to data leakage and missed opportunities. VHomifi was engineered to solve this "Intelligence Gap." It is a centralized nervous system that manages your most valuable assets: your people and your customers.

Module 1

Advanced Human Resource Management (HRM)

VHomifi's HRM module is not just a digital filing cabinet; it is a proactive management tool.

  • Automated Payroll Engine: Handle complex tax structures, variable bonuses, and multi-currency payouts with a single click.
  • Predictive Retention Analytics: Using ML algorithms to identify patterns in employee behavior and predict turnover risks before they happen.
  • Lifecycle Management: From the first "Hello" in the interview to the final "Goodbye" in the exit interview, every touchpoint is recorded.
  • Performance Benchmarking: Shift from subjective reviews to data-driven performance metrics with real-time KPIs.
Module 2

High-Velocity Customer Relationship Management (CRM)

The CRM component of VHomifi is built for teams that refuse to slow down.

  • Lead Scoring AI: Automatically rank leads based on engagement history and conversion probability.
  • Pipeline Visualization: A 3D-inspired Kanban board that allows you to see the health of your sales funnel at a glance.
  • Integrated Communication: Sync emails, calls, and meetings directly into the client profile so no context is ever lost.
Examination

TestOrbit

Uncompromising Integrity. Unlimited Scalability.

The Mission

Education and certification industries are facing a crisis of trust. As testing moves online, the risk of malpractice increases. TestOrbit is our response — a secure, proctored environment that ensures the person taking the test is who they say they are, and the results they achieve are 100% legitimate.

Core Technical Features

AI-Enhanced Proctoring

Using computer vision to detect unauthorized aids, multiple faces, or suspicious behavior during the exam.

Dynamic Question Shuffling

Our "Orbit Engine" ensures no two students ever receive the exact same test in the same order.

Browser Lockdown

The candidate's device is locked into the portal, preventing navigation to search engines or other applications.

Real-Time Latency Optimization

Built with high-concurrency architecture to support 20,000+ simultaneous users without a microsecond of lag.

Automated Parent Alerts
